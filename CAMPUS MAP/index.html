Campus Navigator Guide

Navigation

Select your Start and Destination from the top bar. The optimal path will highlight instantly.

Explore

Click any building or its label to view stunning galleries. Browsing made simple.

Desktop

Zooming: Use your mouse scroll wheel or the +/- buttons. Exploration: Click and drag to pan across the campus map. Interaction: Click any building to view its photo gallery.

Mobile

Navigation: Swipe with one finger to pan the map view. Pinching: Use two fingers to zoom in and out smoothly. Quick Zoom: Double-tap anywhere to zoom in instantly.

Gallery

Browse thumbnails and click to view full-screen. Navigate with Arrows. Zoom with mouse wheel.

Shortcuts

Esc: Close. ← →: Browse. +/-: Zoom. A: Auto-Fit view.
